Anapest Generator: a JavaScript Poem Maker

An anapest, or anapestic meter, is a metrical foot consisting of two unstressed syllables followed by a stressed one. Perhaps the most famous example of an anapestic poem (also mentioned in my article on poetry) is Lord Byron‚Äôs ‚ÄúThe Destruction of Sennacherib‚Äù.

Like the leaves of the forest when Summer is green,
